```bash

```


The app will automatically open at `http://localhost:5173`. If it doesn't, just copy that address into your browser.

That's all! The app should now be running on your computer.

## ğŸ’¡ How to Use the App

1. **Add a Task**: Click the "+ Add New Task" button at the top, fill in the task name and description, then click "Add Task"

2. **Edit a Task**: Click the edit icon (pencil) next to any task, make your changes, and click "Save"

3. **Complete a Task**: Click the "Complete task" button on any pending task. A popup will ask you to confirm.

4. **Delete a Task**: Click the delete icon (trash) next to any task. You'll be asked to confirm before it's deleted.

5. **View History**: Click "History of Completed Tasks" to see all the tasks you've completed

6. **Clear Completed**: Click "Clear Completed Tasks" to remove all completed tasks at once
